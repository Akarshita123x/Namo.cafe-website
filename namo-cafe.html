Our Story

A Passion Brewed into Every Cup

Namo Cafe was born from a simple dream — to create a sanctuary where the aroma of freshly ground coffee mingles with laughter, warmth, and belonging. Nestled in the heart of the city, we opened our doors in 2018 with nothing but passion and a copper espresso machine.

Every bean is carefully sourced from small farms across India, Ethiopia, and Colombia. Our bakers arrive before dawn to ensure your morning begins with something truly made with love.
